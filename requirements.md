

**Problem Statement**

Many community members face difficulty accessing reliable public service information due to fragmented platforms, lack of awareness, and language or digital barriers. This results in delays, misinformation, and exclusion of underserved communities.

**Proposed Solution**

An AI-powered community access platform that provides centralized public service information, intelligent assistance through an AI chatbot, and real-time updates in a simple and inclusive manner.

**Functional Requirements**

Users must be able to register and log in

Users must be able to search and access public services

AI chatbot must answer user queries related to services

System must provide alerts and announcements

Admin must be able to update service information

**Target User**

Citizens, students, rural and underserved communities, NGOs, and local administrators.

**Dependencies / Tools**

AI/NLP models, cloud infrastructure, public datasets, APIs, web or mobile application framework.

**Impact / Goal**

To improve accessibility, reduce information gaps, empower communities, and enhance transparency in public services.
